How we Work

We provide advice and assistance in diplomatic strategy and technique to public, government, political groups, international corporate organizations that are committed to human rights, democracy and international law, using a variety of working methods.

Work Philosophy

New Public Governance
NPG is new trend regime that intertwine the relationship of government with public and private sector for service provision. Key-players of the new regime are state agencies, embassies, I/NGOs, civil society and business entities. Their interests is changing as global agenda and their priorities shift. In this exchanging and competitive environment, public interest is volatile. As a global strategist, we work-out for better ways to communicate strategy and politics. Relations and Diplomacy
The behavior of any states and the statesmen is determined by the national interest. Including national security, political stability and rising economy also define national interest. Yet the central idea of national interest reposes on public welfare and fair economy. When power and politics come to play and money becomes the way, IRD introduces a win-win situation for all parties. Our professional diplomats help building meaningful relation between man and man and between nation and nation. Global Agenda
What is at the center of global agenda? Is it development, environment, data, trade, or war and peace? Global agendas surface as plan, policy and action come into operation. National and global power and responsibilities are determined by territory, jurisdiction and laws of general application. These also affect the conduct of the state and inter-governmental organization. Legal experts at GS are better equipped with the knowledge of human rights, environment, energy, investment and trade law.
